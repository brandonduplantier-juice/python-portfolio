Hospital Readmission Prediction Model

Random Forest classifier predicting 30-day readmission risk using 8 patient features. Extends the descriptive dashboard analysis into predictive modeling. AUC 0.587 on a 300-patient synthetic dataset with class imbalance handling. Risk score at discharge and followup scheduling emerged as strongest predictors.
